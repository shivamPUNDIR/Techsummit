FAQ

It’s a big universe, but we have answers for you. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, send an email to events@github.com.

When is Universe?

October 16-17, 2018. To find more information about when conference activities are happening, see the Universe Schedule and our Beyond Universe events.

Where is Universe?

Palace of Fine Arts
3601 Lyon Street
San Francisco, CA 94123

Can I get a receipt for my ticket?

Yes. To find your receipt:

  • Log in to your Eventbrite account
  • Click Tickets in the drop-down menu on your profile photo in the top-right corner
  • Click GitHub Universe 2018
  • Click Print Tickets to download and print your ticket confirmation

Can I cancel my ticket and get a refund?

Yes. You can get a refund if you complete the request process on or before October 9. To request a refund:

  • Log in to your Eventbrite account
  • Click Tickets in the drop-down menu on your profile photo in the top-right corner
  • Click GitHub Universe 2018
  • Click Request a Refund and complete the form

Can I buy a ticket for just one day?

No. We only have two-day tickets available.

Will the conference have WiFi?

Yes.

Will the conference have captioning or language translation?

We’ll provide live captioning for all Universe content. We won’t have language translation available for the event.

Will there be a nursing room at the venue?

Yes. We’ve arranged a private room for nursing, and we can provide refrigeration upon request.

Will there be gender-neutral bathrooms at the venue?

Yes.

Will there be a place for me to store my things at the venue?

We’ll have a coat check area you can use to drop off luggage, but please don’t leave valuables.

Where do I go if I lose something?

If the event is in progress, stop by the Help Desk to check if someone dropped off your lost item. After the event, send us a note with a description of the item.

Will the event be recorded?

Yes. Session recordings will be available a few weeks after the event.

Do you accommodate special dietary needs?

We provide vegetarian, vegan, dairy-free, and gluten-free food options. To see the options, take a look at our Universe 2018 menu. If you have additional dietary needs, let us know at least 48 hours in advance, and we’ll do our best to provide an option that works for you.

Will the conference have food?

Yes. You’ll have several food options for breakfast and lunch on both days of the conference. You’ll also have several snack options from our event caterers and from some of our event sponsors. Dinner will not be provided.

When is the closing reception?

October 17 5:30-8:30pm

Where is the closing reception?

The Palace of Fine Arts

Do I need a conference ticket to attend the closing reception?

Yes. You’ll need your Universe badge to attend the closing reception.

Will non-alcoholic beverages be available?

Yes.

Does Universe have a Code of Conduct?

Yes. We recommend attendees take a few minutes to review the Code of Conduct. If you’ve got an urgent concern or question, reach out to us by email or give us a call at (415) 842-2837.

Does Universe have Event Terms?

Yes. We recommend attendees take a few minutes to review the Event Terms. If you’ve got an urgent concern or question, reach out to us by email or give us a call at (415) 842-2837.

Could a photo of me at the event be used in promotional materials?

Yes. We occasionally use photos taken at Universe by our photographers in promotional materials.

What should I do if I don’t want my photo taken?

Inform our Registration Team when you check in, and they’ll give you a red lanyard to let our photographers know.
